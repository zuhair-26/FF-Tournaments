About BattleZone

Pakistan's premier Free Fire tournament platform where gamers become champions

Our Story

Founded in 2025, BattleZone emerged from a shared passion for competitive gaming and the desire to create a platform where Free Fire players could showcase their skills and earn real rewards.

What started as a small community tournament organizer has grown into Pakistan's most trusted Free Fire gaming platform, hosting daily competitions with thousands of participants.

We believe in fair play, transparent transactions, and creating opportunities for gamers to monetize their skills. Our mission is to build the ultimate competitive ecosystem for Free Fire in Pakistan.
